About

Welcome back to the halls of the CalArts Graphic Design Program, newly digitized and soaked in #0000ff blue. After over a year of physical distance, we wanted to build this website as an attempt to recapture a fragment of the magic that lives in these halls you know (or don’t)… Instead of presenting the End of Year Show as a direct gallery, our dedicated team of digital craftspeople have meticulously redrawn some of the most meaningful areas of the design department and pinned the work of our peers across the halls, studios, and production spaces we remember so dearly. We’re so excited for you to explore this space, and hope to see you all very soon.
